## How to Be Listed

To be added to this list:
1. Make a contribution (data, code, documentation, or translations)
2. Open a pull request
3. We'll recognize you here and in GitHub's contributors graph

## Recognition Tiers

- **🌱 Seedling**: 1-5 contributions
- **🌿 Sapling**: 6-25 contributions
- **🌳 Tree**: 25+ contributions
- **🌲 Forest**: Sustained contributions over 6+ months
